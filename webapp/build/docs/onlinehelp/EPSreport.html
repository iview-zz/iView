Reports for EPS Product Category
Cyberoam provides reports for various product categories including web server.
 
EPS reports help determining various threats imposed on the network from the end-points using different devices and applications. These reports provide comprehensive overview of malicious files along with their location, most traffic generating applications, usage of USB devices, Internet access reports and email scanning reports. These reports provide a basis for fine-tuning the configuration to better control traffic flow.
