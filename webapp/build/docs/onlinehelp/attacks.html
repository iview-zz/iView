Reports for UTM Product Category : Attacks
Attacks
Attacks reports provide an insight of the attack attempts in your network. It provides complete statistics about the attacks and attackers with concise reports on victims and applications through which the attack was launch. It also gives status about detected attacks and traffic dropped by each device.
 
These reports can facilitate you in determining the severity of the attack and thus provides the basis for strengthening the rules.
 
View attacks reports from Reports → Attacks.
 
It enables to view the break up for various attacks as:
•
Severity wise break-down
•
Top Attacks
•
Top Attackers
•
Top Victims
•
Top Applications used by Attacks
