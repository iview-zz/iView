Trend Reports
Trend reports provide snapshot of various events taking place in the network along with the time of event. It helps network administrator to interpret the pattern of the network activities.
 
The administrator can use the trend reports to monitor most traffic generating events and respective time interval. The administrator can use the trend information to fine-tune various internet policies to control network traffic.
 
Trends for all the product categories are available in the form of graph and table. The administrator can view details of the graph generating data by keeping mouse pointer on the graph.
