Reports for UTM Product Category : Internet Usage
Internet Usage
Internet usage reports provide snapshot of internet traffic in terms of data transfer through your network . It helps identify high volume traffic generators who are affecting overall network traffic. It provides statistics based on traffic generated by various users and user groups.
 
These reports can help determining Internet traffic behaviors and provide a basis for fine-tuning the configuration to better control traffic flow.
 
View mail usage reports from Reports → Internet Usage
 
It enables to view followin reports:
•
Top Data Transfer
•
Top Surfing Time
•
Date wise Usage Summary
•
Exclusive Report by Group
