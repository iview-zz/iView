Reports for UTM Product Category : IM Usage
IM Usage
IM usage reports provide snapshot of Yahoo and WLM Instant Messenger based traffic through your network . It helps identify high volume traffic generators who are affecting overall network traffic. It provides statistics based on traffic generated by instant messengers.
 
These reports can help determining IM traffic behaviors and provide a basis for fine-tuning the configuration to better control traffic flow.
 
View mail usage reports from Reports → IM Usage

•
