Reports for EPS Product Category : eMail Scanning
eMail Scanning
eMail Scanning reports provide the snapshot of email traffic reaching to the end point. EPS product scans incoming and outgoing emails for viruses at end points.
It provides traffic statistics based on hosts and users along with respective actions performed on the emails.
 
These reports can help determining network resource utilization and provide a basis for fine-tuning the configuration to better control traffic flow.
 
View email scanning reports from Reports → eMail Scanning.
 
It enables to view traffic generated by:
•
Top Host
•
Top User
•
Top Action
